About

As a child, my ideal profession was a mechanic because I liked "building things." I studied mechanical engineering with a focus in robotic systems. However at some point, the software side of robotics started interesting me more, and I realized building products doesn't have to only be physical! The speed of software innovation is fascinating, and that led me to the route I'm on.

Currently, I'm a product manager building solutions that customers need (and hopefully love!) in the FinTech space in NYC, creating new products and managing multi-billion dollar ones. I'm a fiend for knowledge, and am always trying to learn or do something new. AI, finance, education and digital consumer products are some professional interests of mine while adventure sports, photography, non-fiction reading and (extremely amateur) painting are things I enjoy doing.

This is my "thinking-out-loud" diary. I believe writing is incredibly important (as a PM and personally) and this is a way to promote that.
